# StashFlow Gestures & Controls

## Global Gestures
*   **Shake to Randomize:** If enabled in settings, physically shaking the device will navigate to a random item based on the current active tab (Random Scene, Random Performer, Random Studio, Random Tag, or Random Gallery).

## Video Player Controls
The video player offers two configurable seek interaction modes:
*   **Drag to Seek:** Swipe horizontally across the video player to scan backward or forward.
*   **Double-tap to Seek:** Double-tap the left or right side of the video player to skip backward or forward by a set duration.

## Keyboard Shortcuts (Desktop/Web)
*   **Number Keys (`1`-`9`):** Pressing a number key quickly switches between the visible navigation tabs.
*   **Focus Management:** The UI captures focus automatically on desktop to ensure shortcut bindings remain active.
